# Experiment 1 — Test Scene Overfitting

## Hypothesis

Robot foundation models may overfit to the specific scenes, backgrounds, or objects present in their evaluation benchmarks, inflating reported performance metrics.

## Goal

Determine whether model performance degrades significantly when evaluated on out-of-distribution scenes versus scenes seen during training/evaluation, and whether reported benchmark numbers reflect genuine generalization.
